Learn About Us

A Non-Profit charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
A Small group now turned into the group of 100+ who wants to be a part of this impact. This idea is once called as ORPD (One Rupee Per A Day) started by our group of B.Tech friends in 2016 had turned into the non-profit charity team in Eluru to start this helping hands team. Intially we just collected small amounts from different classes in our college and help people on roads with blankets and food packets. Later on after enginnering we started collecting in huge amounts (this happened all with your continoues support) and started helping the malnutritioned childern and adults and orphanages.
HopefulPlateProviders mission to create a impact in the needy people lives that no childern or an adult in our surroundings should sleep with hungry stomach and should have minimum basic necessities.
Our vision is to expand this charity to other cities and help the people who're are in need. As a saying "దైవం మానుష రూపేణ" ("God revealed through human form"). We want to prove that it's true.
